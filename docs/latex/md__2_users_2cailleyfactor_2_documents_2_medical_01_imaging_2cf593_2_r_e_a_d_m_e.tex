The coursework seeks to use a \doxylink{class_u_net}{UNet} to segment the lungs from computational tomography images of twelve cases from the Lung CT Segmentation Challenge from the Cancer Imaging Archive. A PDF of a report describing the project in detail is provided in the report folder in the root directory.
